I am an incoming Political Science PhD student. I am interested in government intervention in social media in the MENA region. Methodologically, I am interested in studying how researchers can use large language models in a reproducible and statistically robust manner in the social sciences.

I earned my Bachelors in Political Science from Cairo University in 2025. Previously, I was a [Sawiris Scholar](https://college.uchicago.edu/people/mohamed-sabry-amer) during the 2024-2025 academic year at The University of Chicago where I studied data science and empirical social science. During the summer of 2025, I was part of a virtual exchange program ([MO:VE](https://www.uni-marburg.de/en/studying/incomings/move)) at The University of Marburg where I studied machine learning. Following graduation, I was a Research Associate at J-PAL MENA where I worked on randomized control trials (RCTs).
